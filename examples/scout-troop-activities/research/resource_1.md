# 99 Epic BSA Activities: Scouts Share Their Favorite Troop Adventures

**Source:** https://scoutsmarts.com/best-troop-activites/

## Extracted Information

A crowdsourced list of 99 favourite scouting activities from real Scouts BSA members. Categories span water activities, camping/survival, high adventure, competitions, service, and more.

### Water Activities
- Horseback riding at camp
- Rowing on pristine lakes with friends; patrol leadership experience
- Whitewater canoeing on rapids
- Mile swim distance achievement
- 50-mile canoe trek (multi-day high adventure)
- Kayaking merit badge
- General canoeing
- White water rafting (commercial trips)
- SCUBA diving (Camp Easton)
- Canoeing and snorkeling combined
- Kayak jousting (competitive water sport)
- Sea Base sailing (ocean sailing high-adventure program)
- Manatee swimming and kayaking (Crystal Rivers, Florida wildlife encounter)
- Swimming activities: diving board, annual swim tests, polar bear swimming

### Camping & Survival
- Quinzee snow shelter building and sleeping in them
- Snow camping (winter wilderness)
- Beach camping with ocean sounds
- Wilderness survival nights: building shelters with friends
- Tarp camping at OA ordeal (minimal gear)
- Canoe camping to remote campsites
- Patrol camp: small group camping with accountability and teamwork
- Wilderness survival in extreme cold (9 degrees F with frozen supplies)
- Sierra wilderness survival: building fires without matches in mountains

### High Adventure
- Philmont Trek (high-adventure backpacking, famous ranch)
- Backpacking trips: Harriman State Park NY, Fire Island NY (20 miles)
- Northern Tier Ely Base: week-long canoe expedition with pictographs, waterfalls, border crossings
- Grand Tetons high adventure: week-long Wyoming hiking trip
- Oregon Coast backpacking: week-long Fort Stevens to Cannon Beach trek
- Sierra backpacking: 7-day mountain range expedition
- Cavalcade at Philmont: mounted animal expedition
- High rope course zipline at Scout camp

### Competitions & Games
- Fire building competitions
- Open-fire cooking competitions
- Catapult building (engineering and construction)
- 2-Man saw competition ("Mountain Man Rendezvous" camporee event)
- Lashing competition (rope-tying NYLT event)
- Klondike derby: homemade carts racing, rifle/shotgun range
- Dutch oven cook-offs (competition cooking for parents)
- Backpacking with skill games: relay Scout skills competitions

### Shooting Sports
- Shotgun shooting / marksmanship
- Rifle/shotgun range activities

### Crafts & Skills
- Metal-working
- Knot tying
- Fire building (for ceremonies and campfires)
- Orienteering (navigation and map skills)
- Cooking on camping trips (youth cooking during outdoor trips)

### Rock Climbing & Rappelling
- Rock climbing and rappelling: 100-foot natural rock descent

### Service & Leadership
- Eagle Scout Project (leadership service project)
- Beet-harvesting service project (agricultural community service)
- Cub Scout day camp assistance
- Merit badge instruction: teaching other Scouts
- NYLT (National Youth Leadership Training)
- Order of the Arrow activities

### Social & Ceremonies
- Summer camp (week-long quality time with troop)
- Court of Honor ceremonies (recognition)
- Campfire hosting and leading
- World Jamboree (international Scout gathering)
- Pen pal exchanges with Scouts met at camp

### Urban / Non-Traditional
- Urban hiking: 15-mile, 8-hour city historical tour including cemetery, historic school, mansion
- Mountain biking on varied terrain
- Snowshoe hiking with homemade equipment
- Night hike with Cub Scouts
- Cave trekking (underground exploration)
- Fishing
- Submarine overnight (USS Silversides, Muskegon MI)
