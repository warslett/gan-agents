# 30 Boy Scout and Girl Scout Game Ideas

**Source:** https://www.signupgenius.com/resources/scout-game-ideas

## Extracted Information

30 game ideas organised by age group, covering indoor and outdoor activities suitable for scout meetings and events.

### For Younger Scouts (Ages 5-7 / Daisies and Tiger Cubs)

1. **Introduction Penny Bingo** - Participants collect signatures on bingo cards, then play bingo with pennies as markers. Good icebreaker.
2. **Laser Beam Streamers** - Navigate through a room filled with taped streamers without touching them. Indoor agility challenge.
3. **Garbage Guy** - Pairs collect trash in designated zones while learning environmental stewardship.
4. **Nature Hunt** - Collect specific natural items from outdoors (leaves, pinecones, flowers, etc.).
5. **Freeze Dance** - Dance to music, then freeze when it stops; silliest pose wins.
6. **Cake Walk** - Walk in a circle on numbered floor spaces; randomly drawn numbers determine elimination.
7. **Scrapbook** - Decorate photos from past scouting activities and bind with yarn.
8. **Limbo** - Use a pool noodle as limbo stick; winners clear increasingly lower heights.
9. **Drawing Puzzle** - Create nature artwork, cut into pieces, swap puzzles, and reassemble.
10. **Floor Memory Game** - Match pairs of picture cards spread on the floor; timed competition.

### For Older Scouts (Ages 8+ / Brownies, Juniors, Cadettes, Seniors, Cubs, Boy Scouts)

11. **Photo Booth** - Take silly photos with props and scenery backdrop; share with families.
12. **Sentence Scramble** - Arrange scrambled motto words in correct order.
13. **Minute to Win It Games** - "Junk in the Trunk" challenge: shake ping pong balls from a tissue box.
14. **Tic Tac Toe Trivia** - Answer trivia questions to earn X or O placements on game board.
15. **Fashion Show** - Create outfits for dolls using aluminium foil; award various categories.
16. **Remote Control Car Race** - Race cars through chalk-drawn courses with obstacles.
17. **Capture the Flag** - Team-based outdoor game with zones, flags, and "jail."
18. **Kick the Can** - Classic game where hidden players try to kick a can to free jailed teammates.
19. **Night Sky** - Draw and label constellations on clipboards; top effort earns extra s'mores.
20. **Birding** - Identify native birds during hikes using binoculars.
21. **Nighttime Hike** - After-dark hiking experience for older teens, recognising bravery and citizenship.
22. **Flashlight Tag** - Tag players using flashlights at night; tagged players go to "jail."
23. **Catch and Release Fireflies** - Gently capture fireflies in containers for observation.
24. **Head Bands** - Ask yes/no questions to identify words taped to your forehead.
25. **Squirt Gun War** - Water-based outdoor game; winter alternatives include silly string or Nerf guns.
26. **Teach a Craft, Game, or Survival Skill** - Selected scouts teach their favourite activity to others.
27. **Photo Scavenger Hike** - Photograph specific nature items during hiking; prizes for completed albums.
28. **Make a Public Service Announcement** - Film PSAs about topics of concern; awards for creativity.
29. **Make a Commercial** - Produce sales pitch videos; focuses on creativity and presentation.
30. **Pin the National Park on the State** - After viewing national parks content, place parks on correct states on a wall map. Geography learning game.
