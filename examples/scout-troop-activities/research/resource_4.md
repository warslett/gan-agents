# 15 Teamwork Activity Ideas for Scouts

**Source:** https://moneyminder.com/blog/15-teamwork-activity-ideas-for-scouts/

## Extracted Information

15 team-building activities designed specifically for scout troops, focusing on cooperation, communication, and group problem-solving.

1. **Positive Plates** - Participants wear paper plates on their backs while others write encouraging messages about them with markers, then read the compliments aloud. Builds self-esteem and positive group culture.

2. **Marshmallow Build** - Teams construct towers using marshmallows and toothpicks. Each member can only touch their own materials but collaborates on strategy beforehand. Tests planning and cooperation.

3. **Relay Races** - Traditional team races where members complete sequential tasks like running or passing batons. Each person designs one leg of the relay for ownership.

4. **Puzzle Challenge** - Two teams race to complete identical puzzles within a time limit. Demonstrates collaborative problem-solving under pressure.

5. **Cooking Challenge** - Groups prepare meals together using assigned ingredients within a set timeframe. Practical teamwork with a tasty result.

6. **Escape Rooms** - Teams enter locked rooms and must solve puzzles and clues together to escape. Emphasises critical thinking and communication. Can be commercial or DIY.

7. **Human Knot** - Members stand in a circle holding hands with people across from them, then untangle without releasing hands. Classic physical teamwork challenge.

8. **Paper Airplane Challenge** - Small teams design and test paper airplanes, selecting their best flyer to compete for distance. Combines engineering and teamwork.

9. **Silent Lineup** - Scouts arrange themselves by traits (like shoe size) in order without speaking. Promotes nonverbal communication skills.

10. **Water Balloon Toss** - Teams toss water balloons across increasing distances, with players stepping back after each successful catch. Fun outdoor summer activity.

11. **Paper Bag Skits** - Groups create skits incorporating random props from a bag. All team members must be involved. Encourages creativity and inclusion.

12. **Human Pyramid** - Small groups safely form pyramids, considering weight distribution and size strategically. Requires trust and physical cooperation.

13. **Jam Session** - Teams create harmonious music using available instruments or basic alternatives like kazoos. Creative expression through collaboration.

14. **Blindfolded Pictionary** - Illustrators draw blindfolded while others guess. Can use group-specific clues for added challenge. Tests communication.

15. **Jump Rope Challenge** - Multiple people jump simultaneously under a single long rope. Tests coordination and timing as a group.
