# Top 100 Education Startups - Failory

**Source:** https://www.failory.com/startups/education

## Extracted Information

### Top-Tier Startups by Funding

- **Ellucian** ($1.6B funded): Enterprise resource planning software for higher education institutions
- **Vedantu** ($306.2M funded): Live online tutoring and coaching for academic and competitive exam preparation in India
- **ClassDojo** ($191.1M funded): School communication platform connecting teachers, students, and families
- **Lingokids** ($182.4M funded): Playlearning app for ages 2-8 combining STEM, literacy, and social-emotional skills
- **MasterClass** ($461.4M raised): Celebrity-led online classes across diverse subjects
- **Eruditus** ($1.6B in debt financing): Executive education and professional development programs

### Business Model Categories

- **Direct-to-consumer learning**: Epic (digital reading platform for kids), Quizlet (flashcards and study tools), Quizizz (gamified quizzes)
- **Enterprise/institutional software**: Mosyle (Apple device management for schools), Formstack (cloud data solutions for institutions)
- **Professional development**: Platzi (coding and business courses), General Assembly (workforce training and bootcamps)
- **Specialized services**: Hazel Health (school-based healthcare via telehealth), CareAcademy (childcare and eldercare training)

### Emerging Trends

- **AI integration** is a major trend: MagicSchool AI ($62.4M raised), Edify, and SchoolAI are leveraging artificial intelligence for teacher tools, student support, and administrative automation
- **Geographic diversity**: Strong Indian EdTech presence (Vedantu, Physics Wallah, PlanetSpark), reflecting global market expansion beyond US/Europe
- Combined funding across the 100 companies exceeds $8 billion
- Series B/C rounds and venture capital are the dominant funding mechanisms
- Vertical specialization (healthcare education, childcare training, language learning) is a growing pattern
